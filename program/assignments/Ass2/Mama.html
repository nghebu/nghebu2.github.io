Mamas Homemade Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie

Prep and Cook Time:

  • 30 Min-Prep time
  • 10 Min-Cook time
  • 40 Min-Total
  • 8 Servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 4 egg yols, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 6 tablespoon white sugar

Direction

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven 350 degrees F(175 degree C)
  2. Make Lemon Filing: In a medium saucepan...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar,flour, cornstarch, and salt.
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boid
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1//2 of hot sugar mixture.
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick
    • Remove from heat
    • Pour fillinf into baked pastry shell
  3. Make Merigue: In a large glass or metal bowl...
    • Whip egg whites until foamy
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread merigue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until merigue is golden brown
